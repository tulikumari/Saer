About us

South Surrey Airbnb is a short-term and long-term rental management company servicing White Rock and South Surrey. We provide full service management of Airbnb and HomeAway/VRBO rental properties. Let us handle every detail of operating your rental ensuring tenants stay happy and the property is well maintained.

This includes cleaning and laundry service with our partner companies, guest vetting, correspondence and friendly customer service. We also provide restocking of necessary bath and kitchen supplies, calendar and pricing optimization to ensure maximum reservation bookings, and property listing creation and rental management
